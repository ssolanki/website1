Friendships are among the most complex but meaningful relationships in our lives. These unique bonds often run deeper than family ties, and sometimes last longer than our relationships with spouses or lovers.

As we all know that academics are important but friends share an equal importance. We all have our moments with the group, still we spend little amount of time with each other. And even less with our college friends. Thanks to our education system, we are given an opportunity to have another family of friends in college. We share a bond beyond words, a family beyond blood. So don’t waste time, before it all ends let us go on an adventurous trip with our college friends. Yes you heard it right. College trip, an amazing way to have fun and strengthen our connection with our friends. We will not only have fun with friends and group, we will cherish a memory. For those whose lives revolve around academics and pleasing professors, this trip could be an educational one. Just choose a spot.

Image Credit: Leo Hidalgo

Jokes apart, just like an initial interaction in the college; college trip is also an important part of curriculum. If you haven’t been on a college trip then your college life is incomplete. Everyone must have this experience. This is completely time changing experience. This stage in life is the one when you can get best of both worlds. Now is a time to grow up, take charge of your own life, explore new options in life.

Here is a list of things that you can do on a departmental trip:

1. It is a time to pursue your dreams: If you wish to be a travel blogger, travel agent, photographer and never had a time for your hobbies because of peer pressure, this is a once in a lifetime chance. You are with your friends, they support you and there is no best way to start a new chapter in life without your friends. Take some awesome photographs, travel places. You want to be a painter, go to art museum.

Image Credit: Christian Gonzalez

2. Time to make new contacts: This is time be a grown up now. Let us travel new places, meet new people, have some fun, increase our contact list just beyond family and friends. Meeting strangers is always a tricky concept but having new people in life give us a new perspective, a new way of looking at things and situations. And who knows whom we meet, a lifelong friend or a life partner.

Image Credit: Christian Gonzalez

3. Time to show women empowerment: From time to time women are considered the repressed gender of society. By travelling different places, women not only show courage but also break the stereotype thinking of people. The only time to experience this is in college (college trips).

Image Credit: DFID Flickr

4. Time to be a responsible human being: Everyone claims to be a human being but none think about showing humanity. On college trips you can go to under privileged places and explore the problems in other part of world .If you are thinking for joining any NGOs, social group, any administrative government job, this is a perfect timing and good way to start. If you are in some college dance-drama clubs, then you may perform for the masses to spread awareness just like in old times.

Image Credit: Alex Bellink Flickr

5. Time to learn something new: If you are afraid of being alone, forests, animals, new people, darkness (go camping), or food at new places, it’s time to overcome these fears. Explore the culture, food, ethnicity, religion of places you are visiting. There is a peace in every religion, joy in every culture. Feel it.

Image Credit: Ben Eekhof Flickr

6. If you are a shopaholic: Shopping and souvenirs from new places is always a good memorabilia. More places you visit more are the photos for selfies, instagram, snapchat, facebook, vines, dubsmash. Go partying, go to concerts of your favorite band, rock star, singer, superstar.

Image Credit: Ben Eekhof Flickr

Create an everlasting bond with your buddies, learn how to embrace your feelings and connection with your friends. Tourepedia is here for you, Tourepedia makes it all possible, give us a chance to make a world a better place along with you. Let us plan your trips and we will not disappoint you. As we always say, ”It’s not about ideas, it’s about making them happen”.
